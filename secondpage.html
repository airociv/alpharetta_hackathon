OUR MISSION

Atlanta Mission is the city’s largest and longest-running provider of services to homeless men, women, and children, offering food, shelter, addiction recovery, job attainment, counseling, and educational services. Started as a soup kitchen in 1938 to feed people displaced by The Great Depression, the nonprofit has since expanded to serve over one-third of the state’s homeless population. In 2021, the Atlanta Mission served 350,000+ meals, provided 100,000 beds, and offered thousands of counseling sessions.

OUR VISION

Our social workers are available to consult with clients in Find Hope to connect them with appropriate services in the Atlanta metro area. We want our clients to have the essential resources they need to meet their health needs as well as take steps to end their homelessness.
